      №1 (Power supply) The power supply unit is a secondary power source designed to supply the electrical appliance with electrical energy, subject to compliance with the requirements of its parameters: voltage, current. By converting the energy of other power sources.
      №2 (Motherboard) A motherboard, system board, base board, also known as a main board is a board that contains the main components of a computer that provide logic. The board is the basis not only of a personal computer, but also of smartphones and game consoles.
      №3 (processor) A processor is an electronic circuit designed for data processing. Common types of digital processors: Central processor: part of the computer that implements the process of processing information and coordinates the work of peripheral devices
      №4 (RAM memory) RAM is high-speed computer memory designed for recording, storing and reading information in the process of its processing.
      №5 (Drive SSD,HDD) Accumulators are storage devices designed for long-term (which does not depend on power supply) storage of large volumes of information. The storage capacity is hundreds of times greater than the RAM capacity, or unlimited in the case of removable media storage.
      №6 (video card) A video card is an electronic device, a part of a computer, designed to generate and process images and then display them on the screen of a peripheral device. A video card is usually an expansion card and is inserted into an expansion slot, either universal or dedicated.
      №7 Disk drive(DVD) An optical drive or optical drive is an electrical device for reading and writing information from optical media. Optical drives differ in supported laser disc formats, as well as the ability to write to an optical disc.
